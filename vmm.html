View-MasterMIND

Instructions

The game will select a View-Master reel and the pictures from the reel. It will select a secret word from the reel title and it's your job to guess that word. Type your guess to fill the columns and press return to submit your guess. The letters will change colors as follows:

Letter is not in the word at all.
Letter is correct, but in the wrong place.
Letter is correct and in the right place.

Use these clues to try and guess the word!
